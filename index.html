The Evidence Blueprint

Research Grade • Behavioral Health • Real World Data

NeuroBlu is the world's leading real-world evidence platform that is defining the blueprint for real-world insights in behavioral
health.

THE CHALLENGE

Clinical practice is not conducive to capture measurable behavioral health outcomes.

This leads to lack of quantitative & validated outcomes in real-world databases, hindering pharma research, development and commercial efforts.

Research-grade behavioral health Real World Data with
advanced analytics tools

NeuroBlu brings research-grade behavioral health Real World Data with validated outcome measures and
advanced analytics tools in one user-friendly platform to drive pharma’s clinical and commercial success.

Largest longitudinal behavioral health Real World dataset

  • Over 550K patients
  • 50 million rows of data
  • Across 25 sites
  • Collected over 20+ years

Enhanced data with custom NLP

  • De-identified, aggregated and normalized to a common data model
  • Enriched over 3 years with custom NLP to transform psychiatry notes into quantifiable data
  • Continuous data enhancements planned

User-Friendly Analytics Tools

  • Rapid onboarding
  • Augmented by our expertise in Data Science projects

Product Focus

Explore

Explore patient cohorts in an easy-to-use interface, designed for fast and scalable queries.

Analyze

Analyze data with R programming language with an in-built R packages. Write or migrate existing code on versioned datasets.

Visualize

Visualize results of queries and export for reports and publications.
